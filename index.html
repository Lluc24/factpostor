📖 How to Play FACTPOSTOR

🎯 Objective

Citizens: Identify and vote out the Factpostors
Factpostors: Blend in and survive without being caught

👥 Roles

✓ Citizen - Receives a true fact. Discuss it without revealing directly.

🎭 Factpostor - Receives nothing. Must invent a believable fake fact on the spot.

🎮 Game Flow

  1. Setup: Enter player names and start the game
  2. Role Reveal: Pass the device around. Each player taps "Show My Role" privately
  3. Phase 1 - Declarations (2-4 min): Each player hints at their fact without stating it directly
  4. Phase 2 - Discussion (3-5 min): Ask questions, challenge statements, form theories
  5. Phase 3 - Revelation: Each player states their full fact
  6. Phase 4 - Vote: Everyone points to who they think is a Factpostor
  7. Results: Reveal all roles and see the true facts

💡 Tips

For Citizens: Be specific about your fact. Vague statements make you look suspicious!

For Factpostors: Listen carefully to Citizens and build a fact in a similar domain. Stay calm and confident!

🏆 Scoring

  • Factpostor eliminated → Citizens +1 each
  • Citizen eliminated → Surviving Factpostors +2 each
  • Factpostor survives → Factpostor +3 bonus
